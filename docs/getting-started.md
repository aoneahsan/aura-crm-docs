# Getting started

You can be capturing your first session in a few minutes.

## 1. Sign in with Google

Open the app at **[auracrm.aoneahsan.com](https://auracrm.aoneahsan.com)** and choose **Sign in
with Google**. AuraCRM uses Google sign-in on both web and mobile — there is no separate password to
manage.

The first time you sign in, AuraCRM creates your profile automatically.

## 2. Review the consent gate

On first use you are shown a short consent step covering how your data is handled and how optional
analytics work. You must accept it before the workspace opens. You can revisit these choices later
in **Settings**. See [Security & privacy](/docs/security-and-privacy) for the full data posture.

## 3. Set up your profile

In **Settings** you can:

- Set your display name and practitioner details.
- Pick your theme — light, dark, or system, plus an accent colour, contrast, and reduced-motion
  option. Your preferences sync to your profile so they follow you across devices.
- Turn on optional notifications (off by default).

## 4. Choose a plan

New accounts start on the **free tier** with a small monthly allowance of AI runs so you can try the
full workflow. When you are ready for more, pick **Starter**, **Professional**, or **Firm** on the
billing page. Payment is a **direct bank/account transfer** — there is no card processor. See
[Plans & limits](/docs/plans-and-limits) for the tiers, caps, and how activation works.

## 5. Add a client and record your first session

1. Create a client from the **Clients** area.
2. Open **Capture**, tap record, and speak a short update after your session.
3. AuraCRM transcribes it and produces proposals — a stage change, a follow-up email draft, tasks,
   client homework, and any overage invoice.
4. **Review each proposal and approve the ones you want.** Approved emails send; approved invoices
   are created; the client stage updates.

That is the whole loop. Learn each part in the [Features](/docs/features/capture) section.
