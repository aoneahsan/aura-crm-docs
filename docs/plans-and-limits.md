# Plans & limits

AuraCRM's paid work is **AI orchestration** — turning a session into proposals. Your plan sets how
many of those runs you get each month. The manual CRM (clients, notes, tasks by hand) is always
available.

## Tiers

| Plan | Price | AI runs / month | For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | **2** | Trying the full workflow |
| **Starter** | **$49/mo** | **10** | Newly launched solo practitioners |
| **Professional** | **$129/mo** | **Unlimited** | Established, high-earning solo practitioners |
| **Firm** | **$299/mo+** | **Unlimited** | Boutique firms, agencies, and small clinics |

- **Free** includes a small trial allowance of 2 AI runs per month so you can see the end-to-end
  loop before paying.
- **Starter** adds core voice capture and basic CRM updates with 10 processed sessions per month.
- **Professional** removes the AI-run cap and unlocks style-matched email drafting, client progress
  portals, and invoice drafting and sending.
- **Firm** is everything in Professional plus priority support, custom-branded portals, and migration
  assistance.

These caps are enforced server-side, so a run that would exceed your allowance is declined cleanly
rather than charged.

## How billing works

Payment is a **direct bank/account transfer** — there is no card processor (no Stripe, no PayPal).
The flow:

1. On the billing page, choose a plan. AuraCRM shows you a unique transfer **reference** (for
   example, `AURA-XXXXXXXX-PRO`) and marks your subscription **pending confirmation**.
2. Make the transfer via the owner's payment page, quoting that reference:
   **[aoneahsan.com/payment](https://aoneahsan.com/payment)**.
3. An admin matches the incoming transfer to your reference and confirms it, which activates your
   plan.

Because activation is confirmed by a person, there can be a short delay between paying and your plan
turning active — the reference is what ties your payment to your account, so always include it.

## Changing or cancelling

To change tiers, choose the new plan and follow the same transfer-and-confirm flow. For any billing
question, use the support channel on the [FAQ](/docs/faq#how-do-i-get-help) page.
