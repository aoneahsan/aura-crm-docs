# Client portal

Every client can have a private **progress portal** — a simple, branded page that shows them where
things stand between sessions. It is one of AuraCRM's most useful touchpoints, and it is entirely
under your control.

## What a portal shows

You curate what appears. A portal can include:

- Progress highlights and goals.
- **Homework** the client agreed to, which they can check off.
- Key updates from recent sessions.

Nothing is published to a client until you choose to include it.

## Magic-link access

Clients do **not** create an account. You invite a client and AuraCRM emails them a **magic link**
that opens their portal directly. The portal view is anonymous by design — no sign-in, no password —
so it is effortless for the client to open.

## Homework check-off

When a client marks a homework item complete in their portal, the matching task on your side is
updated to done and the change is recorded in the client's audit history. You see progress without
chasing them for it. See [Tasks](/docs/features/tasks).

## Revoking access

You can revoke a portal link at any time. Once revoked, the link stops working and the visitor sees
a clear "access expired" screen instead of any content. This means a magic link is safe to share and
easy to retire when an engagement ends.

## Why it matters

A living progress portal keeps clients engaged between sessions and gives premium practices a polished,
professional surface — which, in turn, tends to drive referrals.
