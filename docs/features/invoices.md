# Invoices

AuraCRM handles the two billing moments a solo practice cares about: **charging for extra work** and
**paying for the subscription**. Both keep you in control, and neither uses a card processor.

## Overage invoices from a session

Many premium engagements are a base retainer plus billed overages. When a session mentions extra
hours or out-of-scope work, the billing agent detects it and **queues a draft invoice** for that
client.

- The invoice appears as a proposal — review the amount and details, edit if needed, and approve it.
- Nothing is billed automatically; an invoice exists only once you approve it.
- Invoices are listed per client and across your workspace, with their status.

## How clients pay

Payment is a **direct bank/account transfer**, not a card charge through a processor such as Stripe
or PayPal. You share your transfer details with the client and reconcile the payment yourself. This
keeps AuraCRM zero-fee on the payment path and puts you in charge of your own banking relationship.

## Your own subscription

Your AuraCRM subscription is billed the same way — a direct transfer to the owner's payment page,
confirmed by an admin, which activates your plan. The full flow, plan tiers, and transfer reference
are covered in [Plans & limits](/docs/plans-and-limits).
