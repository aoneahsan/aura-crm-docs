# What AuraCRM is

AuraCRM is a **voice-first client operations engine**. After a client session you speak a short
update; AuraCRM transcribes it and turns it into a set of **proposals you review and approve** —
an updated engagement stage, a drafted follow-up email, extracted tasks and client homework, and a
queued overage invoice. Nothing is sent or charged on your behalf; you stay in control of every
action.

The idea in one line: **you talk, it does the paperwork.**

## Who it's for

AuraCRM is built for premium, high-billing solo practitioners who lose hours each week to
non-billable admin:

- Executive and business coaches
- Management consultants
- Private-practice therapists
- Concierge physicians
- Independent wealth advisors

## What it does

- **Capture** a session by voice (or type a note manually).
- **Understand** it — transcription plus semantic extraction moves a client through a clear
  engagement stage: Discovery → Proposal → Active Engagement → Renewal Review.
- **Draft** the downstream work — a style-matched follow-up email, professional tasks, client
  homework, and overage invoices.
- **Publish** a private per-client progress portal with magic-link access.

## What it does not do

- It does **not** send emails, create invoices, or move a client stage without your approval.
- Voice capture today is **foreground, tap-to-record**. Fully ambient, always-on background capture
  is a later, revenue-funded phase — not something AuraCRM claims to do yet.
- AuraCRM is built on a **HIPAA-ready architecture**. It is not a certified HIPAA product; formal
  certification (a signed BAA and audit) happens at go-live. See
  [Security & privacy](/docs/security-and-privacy).
